What to consider

During development of your concept, consider the following characteristics and needs of your users:

  1. Resistance: is there a part that doesn't want the target behavior?
  2. Motivation: Why would they need or want the target behavior?
  3. Skills: Are they able to change their target behavior?
  4. Opportunities: Do they know and have time to achieve the target behavior?
  5. Social environment: Does the user's environment accept the target behavior?
